Churros are delicious!

All about churros and why everyone should try one!

A churro is a type of pastry typically served as a dessert or sometimes as a delicious snack. They are served at many different types of food places and fancy restaurants. Churros are a fried dough pastry covered in cinnamon. Some people like to enjoy them with chocolate sauce or whipped cream. If you love cinnamon you will LOVE churros!

    History of Churros

    Churros originally came from Spain and Mexico. Like many other foods, churros have many theories behind from where they came from. Many people believe that they originated from Spanish Shepherds centuries ago. Many other also believe that the Portuguese that brought the churro over to Europe when they were trading with the Chinese.
